About Me

Let me tell you a little bit about myself. I was born in Japan, spent my formative years in the Philippines and Africa, high school in Illinois and Virginia, and university in California - which is where I currently reside. As such, I am a multicultural citizen of the world!

I am a huge nerd. I love tabletop games. Card games such as Magic: The Gathering and board games like Settlers of Catan. I am into video games like Pokémon and indie games like Terraria, Stardew Valley, and Minecraft, although I'm unsure if that last one can be considered indie after being aquired by Microsoft... As you can see, I like games. I like other things too, however. Drawing, swimming, cooking, comic books, movies, and coding, to name a few.

I like solving problems and working in teams, as well as learning new things, such as coding and design. I have always been drawn to creative outlets like drawing, but at the same time I have always had a very logical mind. I think these two traits will serve me well as a web developer.
